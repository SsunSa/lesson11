About Me

Hello, my name is Sarahi Portillo. I'm an aspiring artist and coder who is passionate about creativity and self-expression. I'm currently working on building up my skills to support my future goal of doing art commissions through my own website.

The reason I am taking this course is out of personal interest. I want to learn how to properly set up websites from scratch so I can build a portfolio site and accept art commissions online in a professional and appealing way.

I hope to gain hands-on experience in coding and website development. Although I'm new to web design, I do have experience in other programming languages such as Java, Eclipse, and C++. This class will help me expand those skills into the web world.
